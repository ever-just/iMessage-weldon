## Description

**WELDON.VIP** is a private, secure messaging platform that connects you directly with Weldon through a beautiful iMessage-style interface.

### Features:
• **Private & Secure** - End-to-end encrypted messaging powered by enterprise-grade infrastructure
• **iMessage-Style UI** - Familiar blue and gray message bubbles with smooth animations
• **Real-Time Messaging** - Instant message delivery with typing indicators
• **Rich Media Support** - Share photos, links, and more
• **Push Notifications** - Never miss a message with instant alerts
• **Clean Interface** - Simple, intuitive design focused on conversation

### Why WELDON.VIP?
Direct access to Weldon through a dedicated, private messaging channel. No ads, no distractions - just pure communication.

### Privacy First
Your conversations are private and secure. We use industry-leading authentication (Supabase) and messaging infrastructure (Stream Chat) to ensure your data is protected.
