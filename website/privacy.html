Privacy Policy for WELDON.VIP

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

Introduction

WELDON.VIP ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WELDON.VIP mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name
  • Profile information you provide

Usage Data

  • Messages you send and receive
  • Chat history
  • Device information (for push notifications)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the messaging service
  • Authenticate your account
  • Send you messages from other users
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send push notifications for new messages

Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication: Powered by Supabase with industry-standard encryption
  • Messages: Stored securely using Stream Chat infrastructure
  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access Control: Only you and your authorized chat participants can access your messages

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase

  • Purpose: User authentication and account management
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Stream Chat

  • Purpose: Real-time messaging infrastructure
  • Privacy Policy: https://getstream.io/privacy/

Data Retention

  • Messages are retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete individual messages at any time
  • Account deletion will remove all associated data within 30 days

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

Email: privacy@weldon.vip
Website: https://weldon.vip

By using WELDON.VIP, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
