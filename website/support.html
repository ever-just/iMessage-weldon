Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up", and enter your email and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email.

How do I send images?

In any conversation, tap the photo icon to access your photo library. Select an image and tap send. The app will request photo library access the first time you use this feature.

Are my messages private?

Yes! All messages are encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your conversations are stored securely and only accessible to you and your chat participants.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your profile settings and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

I'm not receiving push notifications

Check your device settings: Settings → Notifications → WELDON.VIP and ensure notifications are enabled. Also verify you have a stable internet connection.

What iOS version is required?

WELDON.VIP requires iOS 15.2 or later.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at support@weldon.vip with details about the issue, including your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the problem.
